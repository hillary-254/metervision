Features

Automatic Meter Reading

Say goodbye to manually reading your meter. MeterVision uses cutting-edge OCR technology to seamlessly capture your meter readings, ensuring accuracy and convenience.


Digital Billing

Receive your bills instantly and securely within the app. No more waiting for paper bills or worrying about lost mail.


Multiple Payment Options

Pay your bills directly through the app using various payment methods like mobile money, debit/credit cards, or online banking.


Track Your Usage

Monitor your electricity consumption over time with detailed usage charts and graphs. Gain valuable insights into your energy habits and identify areas for potential savings.


Set Budget Alerts

Stay on top of your electricity expenses by setting personalized budget alerts. Receive notifications when you are approaching your budget limit, allowing you to take control of your spending.


Multiple Account Management

Manage multiple electricity accounts belonging to different properties within a single app.


Easy to Use

The app is designed with a user-friendly interface that is intuitive and easy to navigate, even for non-technical users.


Secure and Reliable

MeterVision uses industry-standard security protocols to ensure the safety and integrity of your data.
